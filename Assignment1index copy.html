About Us

Located in the heart of downtown La Verne, California, Warehouse Pizza has been in business since 1972. We offer a variety of pizzas, sandwiches, salads, bread stixs & more in a very charming family atmosphere. Enjoy dining on our heated outdoor patio dining room during the lazy summer months, or dine inside and have a drink while watching your favorite sports game one of our plasma tv's. We also offer a variety of video games to test your skills for the entire family. Dine in or take out only.
